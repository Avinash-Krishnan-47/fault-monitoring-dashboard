Industrial Equipment Health Monitoring

About the Website

This website is all about smart condition monitoring and predictive analysis where we predict the health of the industrial equipment whether it is a faulty one or a healthier one in real time . This is done so as to diagonise the equipment in case of any failure in the equipment . It makes use of a Machine Learning Model to predict the health of the equipment and decide if it is a healthier one or a faulty one . This website is crafed specifically for Instrumentation and Production engineering applications .


Users can input key equipment parameters such as temperature, vibration, voltage, and current and learn more about the health of their equipments . A dedicated Visualize section provides graphical insights and historical trends to support data-driven analysis .
